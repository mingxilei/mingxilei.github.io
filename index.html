About Me

I am Mingxi Lei, currently a M.S. candidate in Electrical Engineering at University of Southern California, advised by Prof. Keith Jenkins. I was also a student researcher at the Radiomics Laboratory, Keck Medicine of USC, advised by Prof. Bino Varghese and Prof. Darryl Hwang.

I received my B.Eng. degree at Guangdong University of Technology, China, advised by Prof. Jun Zhang during my final year at college. Before joining USC, I worked as a research intern and student research affiliate at Bio-Totem Tech and Department of Radiology, Sun Yat-sen University Cancer Center, respectively, both under supervision of Dr. Shuoyu Xu.

My research interests include Medical Image Computing, Interpretable Machine Learning and Radiomics.
