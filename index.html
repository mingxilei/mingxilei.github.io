Bio

I received my M.S. degree in Electrical Engineering from University of Southern California (USC), advised by Prof. Keith Jenkins, and my B.Eng. degree in Information Engineering from Guangdong University of Technology, China.

My research interest emphasizes on applications of Machine/Deep Learning on interdisciplinary studies, more specifically on biomedical imaging and biosignal (time series).

*To be joining the Department of Computer Science & Engineering, University at Buffalo, the State University of New York.
