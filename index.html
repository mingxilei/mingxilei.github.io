Bio

I am a first-year PhD student in Computer Science, supervised by Prof. Jinhui Xu. I received my M.S. degree in Electrical Engineering from University of Southern California (USC), advised by Prof. Keith Jenkins, and my B.Eng. degree in Information Engineering from Guangdong University of Technology, China.

My research interest emphasizes on applications of Machine/Deep Learning on interdisciplinary studies, more specifically on biomedical imaging and biosignal (time series).

I am searching for theoretical research topic to solve current challenges when machine learning is applied on medical applications, e.g. privacy, fairness, etc.
