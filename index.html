Biography

I am a MS candidate in Electrical Engineering at University of Southern California (USC), advised by Prof. Keith Jenkins. I did my summer research at USC Radiomics Lab, Keck Medicine of USC in 2019, advised by Dr. Bino Varghese and Dr. Darryl Hwang.

Before joining USC, I received my B.Eng. degree in Information Engineering at Guangdong University of Technology, China, advised by Prof. Jun Zhang. I was also supervised by Dr. Shuoyu Xu during my research internship in 2018, affiliated with Sun Yet-sen University Cancer Center.

My research interest emphasizes on applications of Machine/Deep Learning on interdisciplinary studies, more specifically on biomedical imaging and biosignal (time series).
