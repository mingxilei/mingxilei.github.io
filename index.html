About Me

I am Mingxi Lei, currently a M.S. student in Electrical Engineering at University of Southern California.

I received my B.Eng. degree at Guangdong University of Technology, China. I was advised by Prof. Jun Zhang during my final year at college. Before joining USC Trojan family, I worked as a research intern at Bio-Totem Tech and participated in research in Sun Yat-sen University Cancer Center both under supervision of Dr. Shuoyu Xu. I hold a membership of Dr. Xu's group in SYSUCC after leaving Bio-Totem.

My research interests include Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Medical Image Computing, Radiomics and Compressive Sensing.
